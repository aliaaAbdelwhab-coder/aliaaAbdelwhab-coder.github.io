About My Journey

My journey into Artificial Intelligence started with a strong foundation in Computer Science at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport. During my studies, I developed a deep interest in problem solving, algorithms, and system design. What began as curiosity about how intelligent systems make decisions quickly evolved into a passion for Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

As I progressed, I immersed myself in classical machine learning models, data preprocessing, and neural networks implementation from scratch. Understanding how models learn mathematically and computationally shaped my analytical thinking.

My transition into Deep Learning and Generative AI marked a turning point. From implementing CNNs and RNNs to building Transformer-based architectures, I focused on not just using AI libraries, but understanding how architectures are designed and optimized.

Today, I specialize in bridging AI systems with real-world mobile applications — building intelligent pipelines, AI-powered apps, and automated workflows that transform raw data into actionable intelligence.
